First Aid Kit

  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Nonperishable food to last 3 days (for each person)
  • 1 gallon of water per person (enough for 3 days)
  • Purification tablets
  • Plates, cutlary, and, of course, a manual can opener
  • 3 days worth of clothing per person
  • Eyeglases and/or contacts with contact solution (enough for three days)
  • Nescesary medication
  • electronics and chargers
  • waterproof matches
  • A whistle
  • Strong tape
  • Battery operated or crank radio
